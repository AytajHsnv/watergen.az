GEN-L


Generate up to 5,000 liters of clean water every day


Requiring no infrastructure other than an electricity supply, this plug-and-drink solution is a robust and renewable source of fresh, clean drinking water. It has been designed to meet the needs of villages, factories and off-grid settlements.


To ensure the high quality of the water generated and maintain freshness and purity regardless of air quality, a built-in multi-barrier air filtration system removes micro-particles and organic traces and adds essential minerals. Each GEN-L water generator is built as a modular unit, so, as water requirements grow and change, they can be quickly and easily integrated with other units to create water generation farms.

GEN-L ADVANTAGES



  • Generates drinking water of the highest quality
  • Easy installation, anywhere
  • Complies with all required World Health Organization (WHO) and Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) standards
  • Perfect for use in remote areas – no complicated, costly water-delivery logistics required
  • Ideal alternative to bottled water
  • In most countries, electricity costs plummet at night, thanks to off-peak rates. AWG uses a dual synergy that can drastically reduce water generation costs.
  • Plug-and-drink solution – requires no infrastructure other than electricity
  • Not reliant on ground water – safe to operate in areas contaminated with arsenic and fluoride


Specifications

Water generation capacity: up to 5,000 liters/day
Power: 3 phase 400 Vac /50Hz
Cost per liter: 2-4 cents
Air filtration: multi-barrier air filtration cascade
Power consumption: ~ 60 kW*h
Dimensions (HxWxL): 2.85m×2.23m×2.64m
Water purification technology: based on sediment filtration, mineralization, activated carbon, microbiological treatment by UV lamp
Water generation efficiency: 350Wh/liter
Weight: 2,630kg (when empty)
